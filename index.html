Day one in Red Hat

@Aimee November 14, 2022

In the morning we had an introduction to the company. We played a game as well as having a tour of the building. I was given my own office desk and computer to use for the week.

The first workshop we were given was on logo design. We had to think of ideas for a gamesroom logo. Using a software called Inkscape we then made the logo digitaly.

We were them given a workshop in the terminal and Git. We set up reporitories and get a GitHub account. We learned many commands in the terminal such as: 

cd
cat
git status
I really enjoyed my first day at Red Hat.

Day two in Red Hat

@Aimee November 15, 2022

In the morning we srarted to make a blog using HTML in VScode editor. I edited it to make it my own. we leaned how to change images, text, links and add pages.

At 10:30 we got a talk from Owen on Trello. Trello is a website that lets you be more orginised in work and what you need to do. I added all my activities into different sections on the page.

After break we had a microbit workshop. I already knew a lot about microbit as I used it in my Junior Cert. This was great fun and I made many programmes such as a dice generator, compass and daylight sensor. This was another great day at Red Hat.

Day three in Red Hat

@Aimee November 16, 2022

In the morning we got some talks about people lives and how you can be in different paths but still end up where you want to be. Dominik and Christian went first followed by Kellie after. The message of all the talks was to not give up on your dreams and I thought it was very insporational.

Later we had a big discussion in the conference room by Rachel about college and software engineering. It was very informative and I feel like I know more about what to look into in the future.

At the end of the day we were split up into groups and we had a bridge building challenge. We came up with a design that uses a lot of triangles for support. We had popsticle sticks, pipe cleaners and sellotape to work with. In the end we wrapped the whole thing in sellotape because it was too weak. We placed second overall. I am pretty happy with that.

Today was a lot of fun.
