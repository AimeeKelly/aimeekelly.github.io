About me

I have many hobbies that I enjoy.
Here are some examples.

Piano

I started piano in 3rd class. I am doing my Grade 7 for the piano. I love to play the piano every day. I like to learn my favourite pieces of music from movies, shows or games on it.

Concertina

I started the concertina when I was in 1st class. I play traditional Irish music on it. I have won many competitions including the Waterford Fleadh which let me go to the Munster Finals.

Coding

I started coding in coderdojo when I was seven. Since then I have learned scratch, Appinventor, HTML, CSS, Javascript and Python. My favourite language to code in is Python because I have been learning that for the longest.

Art

I like to draw people specifically in my sketchbook. I draw characters from shows and video games that I like. I also have an art pad so I can draw on my computer as well.
